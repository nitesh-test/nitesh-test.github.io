Problem Statement

  • High Cost of Quality Education: Private coaching and branded ed-tech platforms are expensive.
  • Lack of Flexibility: Students must choose between large generic classes or unaffordable 1-to-1 tutoring.
  • Accessibility Gaps: Rural and low-income students have limited access to quality mentors.
  • Immediate Doubt-Solving: No affordable platforms provide instant on-demand math help.

Solution / Product

  • Hybrid Course (Recorded + Every 3rd Class Live)
    • Full recorded course library.
    • Every 3rd class is live for doubt resolution.
    • Practice questions with video solutions.
  • Batch-Based Courses
    • Same hybrid model with smaller live batches (2 / 4 / 8 students).
    • More personalized mentoring, lower cost per student.
  • Instant Teacher Booking
    • Students can instantly connect with a math tutor.
    • Flexible pay-per-minute model (₹6/minute).
  • Group Study + Mentor Guidance

    • Guided by a mentor at a shared low cost.
